Welcome

Thank you for participating in this research survey. You will be asked to evaluate different styles of AI-generated hotel review summaries to help us understand which summaries travelers find most helpful when making booking decisions. The survey will take approximately 5 minutes to complete.

The survey will start with a few questions about your travel habits and preferences, followed by tasks where you'll review and compare different AI-generated summary styles.

Your responses will remain confidential and will be used solely for academic research purposes. Upon completing the survey, you will receive a payment of $0.50.
